Why TheFeedCo

Small team. Sharp tools. Straight answers.

AI does the grunt work. We do the thinking.

We build with AI at the core — research, drafting, analysis, iteration — which means we move faster and waste less of your money on overhead. Strategy, taste and final judgement stay human. That's the balance most agencies get wrong in one direction or the other.

We never mark up ad spend.

“Your ad budget goes from your card to Meta, full stop. We charge for management, not margin.”

You'll always know exactly what went to reaching customers and what went to us — because they never mix.

You talk to the person doing the work.

No account managers, no handovers, no “let me check with the team”. When you message us, you reach the person building your site, writing your ads and reading your numbers. Questions get answered the same day, not the same week.

About

Built in Pretoria. Built for e-commerce.

TheFeedCo was founded by Lian Claassen in Pretoria, South Africa, on a simple observation: e-commerce brands don't need bigger agencies — they need better ones. Fewer layers, faster tools, one strategy from the first click to the sale.

We're deliberately small, deliberately focused on e-commerce and DTC, and deliberately honest about where we are: at the start, with everything to prove. If that sounds like a risk, read the founding offer above. If it sounds like an opportunity, you're exactly who we built this for.
